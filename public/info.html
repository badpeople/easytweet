Frequently Asked Questions

Why?

I ended up creating a lot of websites/apps/tools where I wanted to automate tweets for statuses, updates, downtime, news, etc. But having to implement an OAuth solution for each one of those things didnt make sense. This was the simplest possible solution.

How do I de-authorize Easy Tweet ?

go to here and click "Revoke Access" under the "Easy_Tweet_API" app
